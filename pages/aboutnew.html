About Us

Blackard farm is a Hobby Farm set in Grandview, TX. It was established in 2017 by Geoffery and Kathy Blackard.Our goal is to teach others how to build a self-sustainable farm, and a connection to were the foods they eat come from. Currently we are focused on the production of fresh eggs, soap making practices, and gardening techniques for North Texas.
